# Acceptable Use Policy

**Version:** v3.1  
**Owner:** Mia Gonzalez (HR)  
**Last Reviewed:** 2024-03-20  
**Status:** Active

## 1. Purpose

This document establishes the acceptable use policy for the organization. It is maintained by the HR department and applies to all personnel and systems in scope below.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to the following groups: admins, all users, contractors, developers, employees.

## 3. Obligations

### 3.1 Backup

Backup and recovery obligations ensure business continuity in the event of data loss.

- **[Mandatory]** Admins must backup.
- **[Prohibited]** Contractors prohibited backup.

### 3.2 HR

HR obligations apply to onboarding, offboarding, and personnel security practices.

- **[Mandatory]** Admins required HR.
- **[Mandatory]** Admins required HR.

### 3.3 Network

Network obligations govern segmentation, firewalling, and perimeter defenses.

- **[Prohibited]** Employees prohibited network.
- **[Mandatory]** Admins must network.

### 3.4 Asset

Asset management obligations cover the tracking and disposal of company assets.

- **[Mandatory]** Developers shall asset. (Reference: FTP)

### 3.5 Data retention

Data retention obligations set minimum and maximum retention periods for records.

- **[Recommended]** All Users should data retention.

### 3.6 Third-party

Third-party obligations govern engagements with vendors and external partners.

- **[Mandatory]** Developers shall third-party.

### 3.7 Api

API obligations govern how internal and external interfaces must be secured.

- **[Recommended]** All Users should api.

### 3.8 Privacy

Privacy obligations govern the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

- **[Mandatory]** All Users required privacy.

### 3.9 Change

Change management obligations govern how modifications to production systems are approved.

- **[Mandatory]** Employees shall change.
