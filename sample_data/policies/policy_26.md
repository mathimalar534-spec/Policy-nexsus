# Business Continuity Policy

**Version:** v3.0  
**Owner:** Ava Johnson (Engineering)  
**Last Reviewed:** 2026-01-30  
**Status:** Active

## 1. Purpose

This document establishes the business continuity policy for the organization. It is maintained by the Engineering department and applies to all personnel and systems in scope below.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to the following groups: admins, all users, contractors, developers, employees.

## 3. Obligations

### 3.1 Patch

Patch management obligations set expectations for applying security updates.

- **[Prohibited]** All Users prohibited patch.
- **[Recommended]** All Users recommended patch.
- **[Mandatory]** Developers must patch. (Reference: DES)
- **[Recommended]** Employees should patch.

### 3.2 Third-party

Third-party obligations govern engagements with vendors and external partners.

- **[Mandatory]** Admins must third-party.

### 3.3 Change

Change management obligations govern how modifications to production systems are approved.

- **[Prohibited]** All Users prohibited change.

### 3.4 Asset

Asset management obligations cover the tracking and disposal of company assets.

- **[Recommended]** All Users should asset.

### 3.5 Access

Access control obligations define who may reach which systems and data.

- **[Recommended]** Admins recommended access.

### 3.6 Vendor

Vendor obligations apply to the selection and oversight of external suppliers.

- **[Recommended]** All Users recommended vendor.

### 3.7 Mobile

Mobile device obligations apply to any device used to access company resources.

- **[Recommended]** Contractors recommended mobile.

### 3.8 Backup

Backup and recovery obligations ensure business continuity in the event of data loss.

- **[Mandatory]** Admins must backup. (Reference: SOX 2002)
