# Asset Management Policy

**Version:** v3.4  
**Owner:** Ethan Walker (Operations)  
**Last Reviewed:** 2025-01-13  
**Status:** Active

## 1. Purpose

This document establishes the asset management policy for the organization. It is maintained by the Operations department and applies to all personnel and systems in scope below.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to the following groups: admins, all users, developers, employees.

## 3. Obligations

### 3.1 Mobile

Mobile device obligations apply to any device used to access company resources.

- **[Recommended]** Developers recommended mobile.

### 3.2 Asset

Asset management obligations cover the tracking and disposal of company assets.

- **[Mandatory]** Employees required asset.

### 3.3 Provisioning

Provisioning obligations define how user accounts are created and deprovisioned.

- **[Recommended]** Developers recommended provisioning.

### 3.4 Monitoring

Monitoring obligations define what systems must be observed and alerted on.

- **[Mandatory]** Developers must monitoring.
- **[Mandatory]** Admins required monitoring.

### 3.5 Network

Network obligations govern segmentation, firewalling, and perimeter defenses.

- **[Mandatory]** All Users required network.

### 3.6 Cloud

Cloud obligations apply to infrastructure and services hosted with cloud providers.

- **[Mandatory]** Employees shall cloud.

### 3.7 Privacy

Privacy obligations govern the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

- **[Recommended]** Admins recommended privacy.

### 3.8 HR

HR obligations apply to onboarding, offboarding, and personnel security practices.

- **[Mandatory]** Developers required HR.
- **[Mandatory]** Employees required HR.

### 3.9 Change

Change management obligations govern how modifications to production systems are approved.

- **[Recommended]** All Users should change.

### 3.10 Endpoint

Endpoint obligations apply to laptops, workstations, and other managed endpoints.

- **[Prohibited]** Employees prohibited endpoint.

## 4. References

This policy's technical baseline is defined with reference to SHA-1 hashing, as adopted at the time of the last major revision.
