# Awareness Training Policy

**Version:** v2.0  
**Owner:** Sophia Chen (Legal)  
**Last Reviewed:** 2025-08-03  
**Status:** Retired

## 1. Purpose

This document establishes the awareness training policy for the organization. It is maintained by the Legal department and applies to all personnel and systems in scope below.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to the following groups: admins, all users, contractors, employees.

## 3. Obligations

### 3.1 Vendor

Vendor obligations apply to the selection and oversight of external suppliers.

- **[Recommended]** Admins recommended vendor.
- **[Mandatory]** Admins shall vendor.
- **[Recommended]** All Users recommended vendor.

### 3.2 Network

Network obligations govern segmentation, firewalling, and perimeter defenses.

- **[Prohibited]** Admins prohibited network.

### 3.3 HR

HR obligations apply to onboarding, offboarding, and personnel security practices.

- **[Mandatory]** Admins shall HR.

### 3.4 Api

API obligations govern how internal and external interfaces must be secured.

- **[Mandatory]** Contractors required api.

### 3.5 Password

Password obligations govern how credentials are created, stored, and rotated.

- **[Recommended]** Contractors should password. (Reference: SHA-1)
- **[Mandatory]** Employees shall password.

### 3.6 Endpoint

Endpoint obligations apply to laptops, workstations, and other managed endpoints.

- **[Mandatory]** Admins shall endpoint.

### 3.7 Monitoring

Monitoring obligations define what systems must be observed and alerted on.

- **[Mandatory]** Contractors required monitoring.

### 3.8 Access

Access control obligations define who may reach which systems and data.

- **[Mandatory]** Contractors must access. (Reference: GDPR 2016)
