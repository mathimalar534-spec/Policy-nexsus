# Privacy Policy

**Version:** v1.4  
**Owner:** Liam Carter (HR)  
**Last Reviewed:** 2026-01-31  
**Status:** Retired

## 1. Purpose

This document establishes the privacy policy for the organization. It is maintained by the HR department and applies to all personnel and systems in scope below.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to the following groups: admins, all users, contractors, developers, employees.

## 3. Obligations

### 3.1 Patch

Patch management obligations set expectations for applying security updates.

- **[Recommended]** Employees should patch.
- **[Prohibited]** All Users prohibited patch.

### 3.2 Encryption

Encryption obligations specify how data must be protected at rest and in transit.

- **[Mandatory]** Admins shall encryption.

### 3.3 Asset

Asset management obligations cover the tracking and disposal of company assets.

- **[Recommended]** Admins recommended asset.

### 3.4 Vendor

Vendor obligations apply to the selection and oversight of external suppliers.

- **[Mandatory]** Contractors required vendor. (Reference: WEP)
- **[Mandatory]** Contractors shall vendor.

### 3.5 Logging

Logging obligations define what activity must be recorded and for how long.

- **[Mandatory]** All Users required logging.

### 3.6 Data retention

Data retention obligations set minimum and maximum retention periods for records.

- **[Prohibited]** Admins prohibited data retention.
- **[Prohibited]** Employees prohibited data retention.

### 3.7 Backup

Backup and recovery obligations ensure business continuity in the event of data loss.

- **[Mandatory]** Developers shall backup.
- **[Recommended]** Developers should backup.

### 3.8 Access

Access control obligations define who may reach which systems and data.

- **[Mandatory]** Admins required access.
