# Password Policy

**Version:** v2.3  
**Owner:** Ava Johnson (Product)  
**Last Reviewed:** 2023-03-28  
**Status:** Active

## 1. Purpose

This document establishes the password policy for the organization. It is maintained by the Product department and applies to all personnel and systems in scope below.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to the following groups: admins, all users, contractors, developers, employees.

## 3. Obligations

### 3.1 Backup

Backup and recovery obligations ensure business continuity in the event of data loss.

- **[Mandatory]** Contractors must backup.

### 3.2 Monitoring

Monitoring obligations define what systems must be observed and alerted on.

- **[Mandatory]** Developers shall monitoring.
- **[Mandatory]** Admins shall monitoring.
- **[Recommended]** All Users recommended monitoring.

### 3.3 Privacy

Privacy obligations govern the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

- **[Recommended]** Developers recommended privacy.

### 3.4 Change

Change management obligations govern how modifications to production systems are approved.

- **[Recommended]** Employees recommended change.

### 3.5 Api

API obligations govern how internal and external interfaces must be secured.

- **[Mandatory]** All Users shall api.

### 3.6 Password

Password obligations govern how credentials are created, stored, and rotated.

- **[Recommended]** Developers recommended password.

### 3.7 Third-party

Third-party obligations govern engagements with vendors and external partners.

- **[Mandatory]** Developers required third-party.

### 3.8 Asset

Asset management obligations cover the tracking and disposal of company assets.

- **[Mandatory]** All Users required asset.

### 3.9 Provisioning

Provisioning obligations define how user accounts are created and deprovisioned.

- **[Mandatory]** Admins shall provisioning.

### 3.10 HR

HR obligations apply to onboarding, offboarding, and personnel security practices.

- **[Mandatory]** All Users required HR.
