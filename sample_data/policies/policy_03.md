# Data Classification Policy

**Version:** v3.4  
**Owner:** Ava Johnson (Compliance)  
**Last Reviewed:** 2024-02-19  
**Status:** Active

## 1. Purpose

This document establishes the data classification policy for the organization. It is maintained by the Compliance department and applies to all personnel and systems in scope below.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to the following groups: admins, all users, contractors, developers, employees.

## 3. Obligations

### 3.1 Mobile

Mobile device obligations apply to any device used to access company resources.

- **[Recommended]** Developers recommended mobile.
- **[Prohibited]** Employees prohibited mobile.

### 3.2 Asset

Asset management obligations cover the tracking and disposal of company assets.

- **[Recommended]** Contractors should asset.

### 3.3 Third-party

Third-party obligations govern engagements with vendors and external partners.

- **[Prohibited]** Admins prohibited third-party.

### 3.4 Access

Access control obligations define who may reach which systems and data.

- **[Prohibited]** Contractors prohibited access. (Reference: DES)

### 3.5 Endpoint

Endpoint obligations apply to laptops, workstations, and other managed endpoints.

- **[Recommended]** Contractors recommended endpoint.
- **[Mandatory]** All Users must endpoint.

### 3.6 Vendor

Vendor obligations apply to the selection and oversight of external suppliers.

- **[Mandatory]** Developers required vendor.

### 3.7 Privacy

Privacy obligations govern the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

- **[Prohibited]** Admins prohibited privacy.

### 3.8 Cloud

Cloud obligations apply to infrastructure and services hosted with cloud providers.

- **[Mandatory]** Developers required cloud. (Reference: GDPR 2016)

### 3.9 Data retention

Data retention obligations set minimum and maximum retention periods for records.

- **[Prohibited]** Contractors prohibited data retention.

### 3.10 Monitoring

Monitoring obligations define what systems must be observed and alerted on.

- **[Recommended]** Developers recommended monitoring.
